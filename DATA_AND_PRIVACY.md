# Data and Privacy Policy

## Local First
All your sensitive data remains on your machine.
- Clipboard history is stored locally in an SQLite database.
- Notes are stored locally.
- Settings are saved to local AppData.
- The AI Agent (ChatFT) runs completely locally (Localhost) using Ollama and Whisper.net.

## Cloud Light
The application connects to Firebase for Authentication and License validation only.
No user-generated content is ever uploaded to the cloud.
